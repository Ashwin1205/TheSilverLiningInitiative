About Us

The Silver Lining Initiative is a project offering local non-profit organizations with free consulting services ranging from technical solutions, funds, volunteer sources or sources of donation. It was started by two students from the University of Texas at Austin who are extremely dedicated to advocate and aid our community through COVID-19, especially non-profits. Essentially as a free service, The Silver Lining Initiative would like to offer as many services as we can in order to optimize daily functions of a non-profit. We hope to make non-profits effective in their operation in order to provide resources for communities, as well as promote community development.
